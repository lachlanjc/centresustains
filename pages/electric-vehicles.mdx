## Local government <Diagram3 />

Through the Department of Environmental Protection (DEP), the following municipalities have begun installing electric vehicle charging stations and/or purchasing electric vehicles for their fleet.

- [State College Borough](https://www.statecollege.com/news/local-news/electric-vehicle-charging-stations-added-to-beaver-avenue-garage,1480787/) installed six level 2 universal charging stations at the Beaver Ave Parking Garage
- [Ferguson Township](https://www.twp.ferguson.pa.us/home/news/new-electric-vehicle-charging-station-installed-0) installed two level 2 universal charging stations at the Ferguson Township Building and replaced a fleet vehicle with a Chevy Bolt
- College Township installed two level 2 universal charging stations at the College Township Building and replaced a fleet vehicle with a Chevy Bolt
- Patton Township installed two level 2 universal charging stations at the Patton Township Building
