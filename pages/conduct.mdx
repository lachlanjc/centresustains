# Code of Conduct

</Box>

<Container

>

Our events are dedicated to providing a safe, comfortable and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of the following:

- gender
- gender identity and expression
- age
- sexual orientation
- disability
- physical appearance
- body size
- race
- ethnicity
- nationality
- religion
- political views

We do not tolerate harassment of event participants in any form. Sexual language and imagery is not appropriate at any time.

Participants violating these rules **may be expelled** from the event at the discretion of the organizers.
